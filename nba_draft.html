Expected value of NBA draft picks using a simple valuation method

Based on 82games.com post

Ratings are based on the following formula:

Rating = points/game + rebounds/game + assists/game

  • Star -- 20+ rating
  • Solid -- 15 to 19.9
  • Role Player -- 10 to14.9
  • Deep Bench -- 5 to 9.9
  • Complete Bust -- less than 5
  • DNP -- (never played in the NBA)

All the averages are averages of the players' averages, not weighted averages or anything like that. I.e. a dude with 1 game has the same weight as a dude with 800 games.
